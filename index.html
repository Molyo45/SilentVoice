🧠 About SilentVoice

Why We Built This

Over 430 million people worldwide have disabling hearing loss. Sign language is their primary communication tool — yet most people around them don't understand it. SilentVoice bridges that gap using ethical, accessible AI.

Our AI model is trained on diverse hand gesture datasets to ensure it works across different skin tones, hand sizes, and lighting conditions — because ethical AI means AI that works for everyone.
